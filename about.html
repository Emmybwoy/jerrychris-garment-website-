About Our Company

JerryChris Garment & Printing LTD, Where quality, style, and innovation meet since 1978.

Who We Are

JerryChris Garment & Printing LTD was founded in 1978 and has become a trusted name in the garment and printing industry with over 47 years of excellence. From humble beginnings with just a small team, we have grown into a leading provider of high-quality uniforms, sportswear, and customized garment solutions.

Our Journey

The company began its journey specializing in uniform production, meeting the needs of schools and organizations. Over time, we expanded into monogramming, embroidery, and printing services, combining creativity with craftsmanship to serve a wider range of clients.

Before the COVID-19 pandemic, JerryChris Garment & Printing LTD proudly employed over 200 skilled workers, a testament to our capacity and commitment to creating opportunities in the garment industry.
